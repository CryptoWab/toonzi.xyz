Which Image Format Should You Choose?

  • WebP: Best for web images. Offers superior compression (30-40% smaller than JPEG) with excellent quality. Recommended for all web use.
  • JPEG: Best for photographs and complex images. Widely supported and good for general use.
  • PNG: Best for images with transparent backgrounds, logos, and text. Preserves quality perfectly.
  • SVG: Best for vector graphics, icons, and scalable designs. Perfect for logos and illustrations.
